The Ambassador Program

This document represents an initial draft intended to stimulate protocol-wide community discussion and governance.

📋 1. Purpose

This document defines the core architecture of the Stables Ambassador program. Our primary objective is to build a professional, incentivized, Fair & Global network of paid Ambassadors to support the growth of the Stables payment accepting merchant network.

Core Principles:

  • Permissionless Entry: There is no acceptance mechanism or gatekeeping; any participant can act as an Ambassador as soon as they register themselves in the protocol.
  • The Human Layer: Ambassadors are the human layer that integrates merchants into the Stables economic loop and ensures a high-quality, trusted directory.
  • Fair & Global Constraint: The economic model is designed to be universally applicable and fair across all geographies, anchored to the protocol's decentralized logic.

💎 2. The Ecosystem: Five Primary Roles

To ensure the system remains balanced and professional, we define five primary roles relating to merchant onboarding:

  1. The Merchants: Business owners who accept Stables. They choose to pay the 16 Big Mac® fee to be "Verified" and listed in the official directory.
  2. The Independent Participant: A merchant or future ambassador who registers directly through the app without an external pitch.
  3. The Direct Ambassador (Active): The participant who performs the actual onboarding or pitch for another merchant.
  4. The Second Level Ambassador (Mentor): The participant who successfully onboarded and trained a Direct Ambassador.
  5. The Council & Treasury: The autonomous heart of the protocol. It collects anchor fees and arbitrage yield to fund community-voted growth.

🌟 5. The Merchant's Choice

Why Stables?

SecureMilitary-grade security

UnstoppableNo frozen funds

Full Sovereignty: You own your bank, your node, your keys, and your customer relationships.

Why Listing?

  • Visibility: Featured on the global Stables map and directory.

Why Not List?

Stables is an open protocol. A shop may decide to accept Stables without being listed for private operations or minimalist setups at zero cost.

💡 4. The 16 Big Mac® Economy

  • Universal Anchor Fee: 16 Big Mac® (Independent Registration).
  • Mentored Registration Fee: 15 Big Mac® (1 BM Discount).
  • Active Reward: 8 Big Mac® (Fixed for Direct Ambassador).
  • Mentor Reward: 1 Big Mac® (For the Mentor).
  • Council Share: 6-16 Big Mac® (Treasury Growth).
  • Settlement: Paid and settled in any token of Stables including Minima (USDs, EURs, CADs, etc.) or xMinima.

💎 11. Council Treasury: Investment & Ownership

The Stables Council Treasury is the protocol's economic engine, designed for absolute transparency.

The Investment Principle

The entire Stables structure is owned collectively by its participants. The Treasury is the property of the collectivity, meaning the 16 Big Mac® fee is neither a payment nor a donation—it is a 100% investment into the Stables infrastructure.

Your investment stays in the hands of the participants and is utilized exclusively for platform stability, arbitrage, and community-voted growth.

🛡️ 12. The Integrity Clause: Risk & Transparency

Stables is a "Ruled by Code" protocol. For early Ambassadors, transparency is critical:

  • The Early Adopter’s Journey: As of V0.0.01, Stables has no established track record. You are a pioneer.
  • The Perception Risk: Ambassadors carry reputational risk. If adoption fails, it may be perceived as a failed experiment.
  • Sovereignty is the Shield: Even if the brand vanishes, you remain in control of your Minima node and private keys.
