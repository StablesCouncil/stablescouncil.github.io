On-Ramp (Paper Money → Stables)

1
🏦 Deposit Fiat
Deposit fiat in one of the partner exchanges (via bank or card).
2
💳 Buy USDT
Trade your deposited fiat for USDT on the exchange.
3
🌉 Optional Bridge
Bridge USDT to MxUSDT via mxusd.global if required by your venue.
4
⚡ Buy MINIMA
Trade your USDT for MINIMA on a CEX or the Minima DEX (see the list of venues below).
5
📱 On-chain Withdrawal
Send MINIMA to your Stables wallet address.
6
✨ Mint Stables
Convert your MINIMA into Stables tokens directly in the app.

Off-Ramp (Stables → Paper Money)

1
🔥 Burn to MINIMA
Burn your Stables tokens to reclaim native MINIMA on-chain.
2
📥 CEX / DEX
Deposit to CEX, or use DEX directly from your wallet.
3
📤 Sell MINIMA
Trade for USDT on a CEX, or MxUSDT on the DEX (see venues below).
4
🌉 Optional Bridge
Bridge MxUSDT to USDT via mxusd.global (skip if already on CEX).
5
🔀 USDT to Fiat
Sell USDT for fiat currency on the exchange.
6
🏧 Withdraw Fiat
Withdraw fiat directly to your bank or card.
