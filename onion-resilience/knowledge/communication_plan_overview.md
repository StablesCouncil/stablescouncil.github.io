# Communication Plan overview

**Live page:** https://stablescouncil.org/communication_plan.html  
**Status:** Drafting

The Communication Plan is the public planning page for how Stables will communicate across platforms and connect communication work to Dashboard success measures.

## Public versus community/member channels

The plan distinguishes public-facing social platforms from community/member channels.

General public channels include platforms such as:

- X
- Instagram
- Facebook
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Twitch
- Rumble
- Moltbook

Community/member channels include:

- Telegram Community
- Telegram Council Official
- Discord working groups

Telegram and Discord should not be treated as general-public broadcast channels only. They are also places where existing community members, possible Council operators, ambassadors, and contributors can engage in more specific discussion.

## Current communication objective

The immediate communication objective is to announce and invite feedback on:

- the Navigation System,
- the Dashboard,
- the Communication Plan,
- the broader Version 0.0.01 | Community Draft framing.

This should be presented as a community draft, not as final structure.

## Channel rules

For X:

- Follow the handshake X/Twitter rules.
- Include required hashtags: `#BYB #Stablecoin #Minima`.
- Keep within 280 characters.
- Use concise language and a clear link.

For Telegram:

- Do not use hashtags.
- Use a warmer explanation.
- Invite comments, questions, and review.

For all channels:

- Do not overclaim automation.
- Do not say the Dashboard has mature production data.
- Do not imply the Council structure is final.
- Do explain that the Dashboard and Navigation System are now public and connected.

## Recommended launch copy base

```text
We published the first Community Draft of the Stables Navigation System and Dashboard.

Navigation System: direction, streams, cards, dependencies, and work status.
Dashboard: measurable success indicators we can improve and automate over time.

This is Version 0.0.01, shared for community review and feedback.
https://stablescouncil.org/links.html
```
