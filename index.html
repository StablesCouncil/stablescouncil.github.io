The operating loop

From self-custody to circulation.

Stables is not only a wallet screen. The product story is a loop: people hold their own keys, merchants accept direct settlement, local exchange makes entry practical, and the protocol surfaces risk clearly.

01

Hold your bank

Start with self-custody, transparent balances, and a clear channel label so users know what is live and what is demo.

02

Pay real merchants

Move from passive holding to direct commerce, where customers and merchants settle without an intermediary account layer.

03

Circulate locally

On and off ramps should lead with trusted merchants and DIY community exchange before technical venues.

04

Show the risk

Coverage, leverage, fees, and governance need visible truth surfaces so the system can be inspected as it grows.
